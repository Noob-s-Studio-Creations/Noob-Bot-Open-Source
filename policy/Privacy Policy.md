# Privacy Policy For The NoobBot

Your Privacy Is Important To Us This Policy Explains What Data **NoobBot** Are Collects And How It Is Was Handled By The Bot Or Us?

---

### 1. Data We Collect
NoobBot Collects Only The Minimum Data Required For It's Functionality Including:
- Server Identifiers (Such As Server ID And Name)
- User Identifiers (Such As User ID And Username) Only When Necessary For Command Execution Or Debugging

NoobBot Does Not Collect Or Store Message Content

### 2. How We Use Data
We Use The Collected Information Solely To
* Provide Music Playback Services
* Maintain Server-Specific Settings (Example Custom Prefixes Or Volume Levels)
* Troubleshoot Bugs And Technical Issues

### 3. Data Storage & Security
- **Temporary Files:** Images Or Media Provided Through Bot Commands Are Processed Temporarily And Automatically Deleted After The Requested Operation Is Completed
- **Storage:** NoobBot Does Not Store Personal Messages Or Private User Data All Processing Is Performed In-Memory Or Via Temporary Storage Only
- **Sharing:** NoobBot Does Not Sell, Rent, Or Share User Data With Third Parties
- **Discord:** As NoobBot Operates On Discord, Discord May Collect Data In Accordance With It's Own Privacy Policy

### 4. Third-Party Services
NoobBot May Interact With Third-Party Services (Such As YouTube Or SoundCloud) To Fetch Media Content
NoobBot Does Not Share Discord User Data With These Services
Any Data Collection Performed By These Services Is Governed By Their Respective Privacy Policies

### 5. Your Rights (Data Deletion)
If You Wish To Delete Your Server Data Or Settings You Can 
1.  Remove (Kick) The Bot From Your Server
2.  Contact The Developer Via Our [Support Server](https://discord.gg/vkFJhzFmYY) To Request A Manual Fully Data Delete

### 6. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy May Be Updated From Time To Time
Significant Changes Will Be Communicated Through The Official [Support Server](https://discord.gg/vkFJhzFmYY) When Applicable.
